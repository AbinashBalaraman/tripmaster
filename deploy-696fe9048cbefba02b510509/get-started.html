How will you use TripMaster?

Choose the mode that best fits your travel style. You can change this later.

Solo Mode

Perfect for personal trip tracking. No sharing needed.

Best for:

  • Personal vacation
  • Business trip
  • Solo adventure

Group Mode

Ideal for one-off trips with friends. Share via invite code.

Best for:

  • Boys Trip
  • Bachelorette Party
  • Family vacation

Full Account

Best for frequent travelers. Manage multiple trips, persistent history.

Best for:

  • Travel Blogger
  • Digital Nomad
  • Travel Agency
